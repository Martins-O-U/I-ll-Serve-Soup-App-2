ABOUT I'LL SERVE SOUP

I'LL SERVE SOUP is here to serve you - literally. All you need to do is simply sign up as a Kitchen Manager to get full access to the inventory management system.

The app gives you editing access to electronically stock your kitchen and sends you notifications when stock levels are running below a set threshold. Even more, sign up as a Volunteer to collaborate with other kitchens around you.
