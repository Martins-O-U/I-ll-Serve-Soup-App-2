Being a Kitchen Manager

What would we do without food?
Yes, you're right - Absolutely nothing! So, being a Kitchen Manager means that you literally hold the key to making everything happen. You're a Superhuman!

If you're like Ann though, you wouldn't be feeling very superhuman. Ann is Senior Chef and Kitchen Manager for Oslo Diner, which is great because we all love Oslo Diner. But, between creating gourmet dishes, managing kitchen staff, keeping inventory and restocking the kitchen, Ann is in an endless roller coaster that has ultimately yielded a decline in the charm and appeal of Oslo Diner. Who wants to travel all the way down to the Diner only to realize the kitchen is out of cheese (again)? Not me, and certainly not 50% of Oslo residents all whom have since switched kitchen choices.


Are you Ann?
It's time to pull the brakes on that roller coaster. In comes I'LL SERVE SOUP App to the rescue! Imagine the ease of managing kitchen inventory, restocking and even hiring kitchen volunteers all in one application and from the comfort of your office - Perfect right?

I'LL SERVE SOUP caters to all your worries as a kitchen manager and even draws customers to your kitchen by showcasing food items you have in stock and directing interested buyers to your kitchen. What is more, you can directly volunteer to serve other kitchens and even benefit from the volunteer actions of other kitchens.
